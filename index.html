How To Play

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.
  3. You win the game by matching the total score to the random number, but if you go over...then you lose!
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on the crystal.
  5. Each time you play, the crystals will have a new value.
